Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements for self-drive rentals?

For self-drive rentals, you'll need a valid driver's license (international driving permit if required), passport, credit card for the security deposit, and be at least 25 years old. Additional drivers must also meet these requirements and be registered on the rental agreement.

Can I cross international borders with the rental vehicle?

Yes, our vehicles can be driven throughout Germany, Switzerland, Austria, France, Italy, and most EU countries. We provide all necessary documentation and stickers required for international travel. Please inform us of your intended route at the time of booking.

What child safety equipment is available?

We offer a range of child safety equipment including infant carriers (0-12 months), toddler seats (1-4 years), booster seats (4-12 years), and ISOFIX attachments. Please specify the ages and number of children when booking so we can provide the appropriate equipment.

What happens in case of a breakdown or accident?

All our vehicles come with comprehensive insurance and 24/7 roadside assistance. In case of any issue, simply call our emergency number, and we'll arrange immediate assistance. If necessary, we'll provide a replacement vehicle to minimize disruption to your family trip.

Can I extend my rental period?

Yes, rental extensions are possible subject to vehicle availability. We recommend giving us at least 48 hours' notice for extensions. Additional days will be charged at the same daily rate as your original booking.
