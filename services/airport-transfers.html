Frequently Asked Questions

Airport Transfer FAQs

What happens if my flight is delayed?

We monitor all flight arrivals in real-time, so we're always aware of any delays. Your chauffeur will adjust their schedule accordingly and be there when you arrive – at no extra charge for standard delays.

How will I recognize my driver?

Your chauffeur will be waiting at the arrivals hall with a personalized sign displaying your name. Additionally, you'll receive your driver's contact details in your booking confirmation.

Is there a waiting time charge?

We include a complimentary waiting time of 60 minutes for international flights and 30 minutes for domestic flights after the scheduled landing time. For pickups from hotels or other locations, we provide a 15-minute courtesy waiting time.

How much luggage can I bring?

Our sedan vehicles can accommodate 3 passengers with 3 standard suitcases plus hand luggage. Our vans can fit more luggage – up to 6-8 suitcases depending on the vehicle size. If you have special luggage requirements, please mention this at the time of booking.

Can I book a child seat?

Yes, we offer child and baby seats upon request. Please specify the age and weight of the child when booking so we can provide the appropriate seat.
