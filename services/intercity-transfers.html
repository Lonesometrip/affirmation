Why Our Intercity Service Beats Other Options

Compared to Trains:

  • No need to reach train stations, which are often in busy city centers
  • No crowded compartments or noisy passengers
  • Direct service without transfers or connections
  • Flexible departure times rather than fixed schedules
  • Privacy to work, rest, or enjoy the journey with your companions

Compared to Flights:

  • No airport security checks or boarding procedures
  • No need to arrive hours before departure
  • Door-to-door service eliminates airport transfers
  • More generous luggage allowance with no extra fees
  • Often comparable or faster total travel time for distances under 500km

Compared to Rental Cars:

  • No need to navigate unfamiliar roads or worry about directions
  • Ability to work or relax during the journey
  • No parking hassles at your destination
  • No concerns about different driving regulations in foreign countries
  • Chauffeur's local knowledge enhances the experience
