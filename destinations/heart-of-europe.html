Sample Itinerary

Our Heart of Europe Tour is fully customizable, but here's a sample 10-day itinerary:

  • Day 1-2: Munich, Germany - Explore the Bavarian capital's rich heritage
  • Day 3-4: Salzburg, Austria - Experience Mozart's birthplace and "Sound of Music" filming locations
  • Day 5-6: Vienna, Austria - Discover imperial palaces and musical legacy
  • Day 7: Bratislava, Slovakia - Enjoy the charming capital on the Danube
  • Day 8-9: Budapest, Hungary - Experience the "Pearl of the Danube"
  • Day 10: Prague, Czech Republic - Conclude in the "City of a Hundred Spires"
