Tour Overview

Discover the magic of Prague, the City of a Hundred Spires, with our luxury chauffeur service. From the historic Charles Bridge to the majestic Prague Castle, experience the rich history and stunning architecture of this enchanting city.

Our tailored Prague experience allows you to explore this UNESCO World Heritage city at your own pace. From the iconic Charles Bridge and Prague Castle to hidden gems off the tourist path, our experienced chauffeurs and local guides will ensure you experience the very best of the Czech capital.

Whether you're interested in history, architecture, cuisine, or culture, our customizable itineraries can be tailored to your specific interests, ensuring an unforgettable Prague experience.

Tour Highlights

  • Visit Prague Castle, the largest ancient castle complex in the world
  • Stroll across the iconic Charles Bridge with its unique statues
  • Explore the charming Old Town Square and see the Astronomical Clock
  • Discover the historic Jewish Quarter (Josefov)
  • Wander through the picturesque Lesser Town (Malá Strana)
  • Cruise on the Vltava River for a different perspective of the city
  • Experience traditional Czech cuisine and famous beer culture
  • Visit the vibrant Wenceslas Square and National Museum
