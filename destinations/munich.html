Tour Overview

Munich, the capital of Bavaria, offers a perfect blend of historical architecture, world-class museums, beautiful parks, and a vibrant cultural scene. Our chauffeur-driven Munich tours provide a comfortable and personalized way to explore this captivating city and its surroundings at your own pace.

Whether you're interested in Munich's rich history, its beer gardens and food culture, or its stunning architecture, our knowledgeable drivers will ensure you experience the best of what this remarkable city has to offer. From the historic center to day trips to nearby attractions, we customize each tour to match your interests.

Tour Highlights

  • Marienplatz & Old Town: The heart of Munich with its magnificent Neo-Gothic New Town Hall, Glockenspiel, and historic buildings
  • Munich Residenz: The former royal palace of the Bavarian monarchs and one of Europe's most impressive palace museums
  • Nymphenburg Palace: The stunning summer residence of Bavarian rulers with its beautiful gardens
  • English Garden: One of the world's largest urban parks, featuring the Chinese Tower, Monopteros, and famous river surfers
  • BMW World & Museum: Futuristic showrooms and exhibitions celebrating Bavaria's famous automobile brand
  • Viktualienmarkt: Munich's gourmet food market with over 140 stalls offering local and international delicacies
  • Olympic Park: Site of the 1972 Summer Olympics with its innovative architecture and observation tower
  • Museums Quarter: Home to some of Germany's finest museums including the Alte Pinakothek, Neue Pinakothek, and Pinakothek der Moderne
  • Traditional Beer Halls: Experience authentic Bavarian culture at famous establishments like Hofbräuhaus or Augustiner-Keller

Popular Day Trips from Munich

  • Neuschwanstein Castle: The fairy-tale castle that inspired Disney, nestled in the Bavarian Alps (2-hour drive)
  • Salzburg, Austria: Mozart's birthplace with its magnificent baroque architecture (2-hour drive)
  • Dachau Concentration Camp Memorial: An important historical site for reflection (30-minute drive)
  • Rothenburg ob der Tauber: Perfectly preserved medieval town (2.5-hour drive)
  • Lake Starnberg: Beautiful Alpine lake with mountain views (30-minute drive)
  • Zugspitze: Germany's highest peak with breathtaking panoramic views (1.5-hour drive)
